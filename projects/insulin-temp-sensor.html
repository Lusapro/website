Insulin temp sensor

A self-made temp sensor to guard the insulin and notify the user in case of freezing.

Attiny85 C++ Soldering 3D print

What I built

  • Small device to have with the insulin
  • LEDs for notification
  • 3D-printed enclosure and internal mounting
  • Wiring, soldering, and iterative prototyping

Challenges

  • Fitting components neatly inside a compact case
  • Reliable button feel and responsiveness
  • Power and stability during gameplay

Quick facts

Status: Prototype
Category: Embedded + 3D print
Main tech: Attiny85, C++
